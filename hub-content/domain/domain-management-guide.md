# Guide: Domain Management

This guide explains how your domain works and how it is managed.

Your domain is your digital address.
Example:
yourdomain.com

It connects your website, email, and other online services.

---

## What this is

Your domain is:

- Registered in your company’s name
- Legally owned by your company
- Managed administratively by Eye On Idea

You remain the legal owner at all times.

---

## What Eye On Idea does

- Registers the domain in your company’s name
- Configures DNS (website, email, security)
- Manages renewals
- Sends renewal reminders
- Handles technical configuration

---

## What you are responsible for

- Paying annual renewal fees
- Confirming renewal when requested
- Informing us if company details change
- Requesting transfers if needed

---

## How domain renewal works

Domains renew yearly.

### Renewal process

1. One month before renewal, Eye On Idea contacts you.
2. Renewal invoice is sent.
3. You confirm and pay.
4. Domain is renewed for 1 year.

---

### If no response is received

In the final month before expiration:

- Weekly reminders are sent
- Contact attempts may include phone and email

If no response is received before expiration:

- The domain may expire
- The domain can become publicly available
- Website and email may stop working

Eye On Idea does not retain ownership of expired domains.

---

## Transferring your domain

You can transfer your domain at any time.

Process:

1. Written confirmation from the domain owner is required
2. Transfer code (auth code) is provided
3. The new provider handles the transfer

Eye On Idea does not block domain transfers.
The domain belongs to you.

---

## DNS and technical configuration

Eye On Idea manages:

- Website connection
- Email records (SPF, DKIM, MX)
- SSL setup
- Security configuration

We recommend not changing DNS settings without coordination, as it may interrupt:

- Website access
- Email delivery
- Security certificates

## Technical Information (Advanced)

### Registrar & Hosting

- Domains are registered via a European domain registrar.
- DNS is configured for EU-hosted website and email services.
- SSL certificates are automatically issued and renewed.

### DNS Records Managed

Typical records include:

- A / AAAA records (website)
- MX records (email routing)
- SPF records (email security)
- DKIM records (email verification)
- DMARC (recommended for security)

### Expiration & Lifecycle

Domains follow a lifecycle:

1. Active
2. Expired
3. Grace period
4. Redemption period
5. Released

Recovery after expiration may involve additional fees depending on timing.

To avoid risk, renew before expiration.
