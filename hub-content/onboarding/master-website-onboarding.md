# Website Onboarding & Launch Guide

Welcome.

Your website is nearing launch.
This document explains what happens next and what to expect.

Please read this carefully before launch.

---

# 1. Where We Are Now

You are currently in the **Pre-Launch Phase**.

You have received:

- Access to the test server
- CMS invitation (if included)
- Review access to the full website
- Final invoice (2–5 business days before launch)

Launch means:
Your website becomes live and accessible through your domain.

---

# 2. Launch Timeline

## Before Launch

You review:

- All page content
- Contact details
- Images
- Legal pages
- Forms
- Navigation
- SEO fields (if relevant)

You confirm approval.

Final payment is due on launch day.

---

## Launch Day

- The website goes live on your domain
- SSL is active
- Hosting is monitored
- Analytics tracking is active (if included)
- All deliverables are handed over

The website will go live even if payment has not yet been completed.  
However, payment terms remain legally binding under the project agreement.

---

## After Launch

You enter a **2-week support window**.

During this period:

Included:

- Small text edits
- Image replacements
- Typo corrections
- Contact info adjustments

Not included:

- New pages
- Layout changes
- Design adjustments
- New features

Major changes require a new agreement or support plan.

---

# 3. Access & Credentials

You may receive:

- CMS access
- Email accounts
- Domain confirmation
- Hosting overview

---

## CMS

- Invitations are sent by email
- Editors can add and delete content
- Writers can add content only
- Content recovery is not guaranteed

You are responsible for managing your own content.

---

## Email

- Initial password provided by Eye On Idea
- You must change it after first login
- Email hosting is EU-based
- Backups are your responsibility

Eye On Idea does not retain mailbox access.

---

## Domain

- Domain is registered in your company name
- You are the legal owner
- Renewal is yearly
- Renewal reminders are sent in advance

Failure to renew may result in expiration.

---

## Hosting

- Website is hosted in Europe
- SSL is active
- Weekly uptime checks are performed
- FTP/server access is not provided by default

DNS records can be provided upon request.

---

# 4. Legal & Compliance
