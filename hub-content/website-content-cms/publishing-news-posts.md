# Guide: Writing and Publishing News / Posts

This guide shows how to create and publish a news article or blog post.

## What this is

Your news/posts section lets you:

- Share updates
- Publish articles
- Improve visibility in search engines
- Keep your website active

Each post becomes its own page on the website.

## What you can do

- Create a new post
- Add images
- Structure text with headings
- Save as draft
- Publish when ready

## What you should know

- Drafts are private.
- Publishing makes the post live.
- You can update a post anytime after publishing.
- Deleting a post removes it permanently.

---

## Step-by-step guide

### Step 1 — Create a new post

1. Open the Studio
2. Click **News** or **Posts**
3. Click **Create new**

**Screenshot:** `cms-news-01-create-post.png`

---

### Step 2 — Add a title

Write a clear title.

Good:

- Short
- Specific
- Easy to understand

Avoid:

- Very long titles
- All caps
- Keyword stuffing

**Screenshot:** `cms-news-02-title.png`

---

### Step 3 — Add a featured image

1. Click the image field
2. Upload a new image or select an existing one
3. Adjust crop if needed

Tip:
Use landscape format unless otherwise stated in the help text.

**Screenshot:** `cms-news-03-featured-image.png`

---

### Step 4 — Write your content

Use headings to structure your text.

Example structure:

- Introduction
- Main section
- Supporting section
- Conclusion

Keep paragraphs short.
Avoid long blocks of text.

**Screenshot:** `cms-news-04-content-editor.png`

---

### Step 5 — Save as draft

Draft saves automatically.
You can leave and return later.

Use draft if:

- You are not finished
- You want internal approval first

**Screenshot:** `cms-news-05-draft.png`

---

### Step 6 — Publish

Click **Publish** when ready.

After publishing:

- The post becomes visible on your website
- It appears in your news overview page

**Screenshot:** `cms-news-06-publish.png`

---

### Step 7 — Review on the live website

Open your website.
Check:

- Text layout
- Image crop
- Mobile version
- Spelling

If needed, return and adjust.

**Screenshot:** `cms-news-07-check-live.png`

---

## Common mistakes

### Writing without structure

Use headings.
Break up long paragraphs.

### Using very large images

Large images slow down loading.
Follow the recommended size in the Studio.

### Publishing without previewing

Always check how it looks on the website.

---

## FAQ

### Can I edit a post after publishing?

Yes. Edit and publish again.

### Can I schedule a post?

If scheduling is enabled in your project, yes.
Otherwise publish manually.

### What happens if I delete a post?

It is removed permanently.

---

## Technical Information (Advanced)

- Posts are structured content documents in Sanity.
- Each post generates a static page when published.
- Publishing triggers an automated rebuild of the website.
- Content is stored in Sanity’s EU-hosted Content Lake.
- Image handling is optimized automatically by the website frontend.
