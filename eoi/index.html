EOI Areas

UX · UI · Accessibility · Usability · Responsiveness · Mobile/Tablet · Cross-Browser · Compatibility · Screen Readers · Performance · Design · Testing · Front-End

EOI is a quality assurance partner that helps businesses across Europe build digital products that are reliable, accessible, and easy to use. We combine usability, accessibility, and front-end QA expertise to make sure your digital presence works for every user.

We work closely with your team to review, test, and improve digital services. Every audit or project is tailored to your needs, with clear and practical steps that can be acted on right away. Our process is flexible, human-centered, and designed to fit into your workflow.

With a focus on detail and measurable results, we help you deliver products that perform better and create smoother experiences for your users. Our goal is simple: to raise the quality of your digital solutions and support your long-term success.

Services built for your workflow

At Eye On Idea, we keep quality assurance clear and practical. Our services are flexible and adapt to the way your business works. Whether you need a quick review, structured project support, or a long-term QA partner, we focus on giving you actionable improvements that strengthen your product without slowing your team down

From short audits to ongoing support, the outcome is reduce friction: digital products that work better for your users and reduce friction for your team. Each service is designed to improve accessibility, usability, and reliability. Explore our service options to see which setup fits your needs best.
